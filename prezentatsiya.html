TADQIQOT MAQSADI VA VAZIFALARI

🎯 Asosiy maqsad:

📋 Vazifalar:

  1. Nazariy tahlil: Ko'p o'zgaruvchili funksiya, aniqlash sohasi va uzluksizlik tushunchalarini bayon etish
  2. Differensiallash: Xususiy hosilalar va to'la differensialning hisoblash usullarini o'rganish
  3. Geometrik ma'nolar: Funksiya gradienti, yo'nalish bo'yicha hosila, urinma tekislik tenglamalarini o'rganish
  4. Taqribiy hisoblash: Teylor va Makloren formulalarini tatbiq etish
  5. Ekstremumlar: Ko'p o'zgaruvchili funksiyalarning ekstremumlarini topish usullarini o'zlash tirin
Tadqiqot obyekti: \( z = f(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_m) \) – ikki va undan ortiq argumentga bog'liq skalyar funksiyalar

XUSUSIY HOSILALAR

Ta'rif:

Agar \( f(x, y) \) funksiya \( M_0(x_0, y_0) \) nuqtada ushbu limit:

$$\lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) - f(x_0, y_0)}{\Delta x}$$

mavjud va chekli bo'lsa, bu limit funksiyaning \( M_0 \) nuqtada x bo'yicha xususiy hosilasi deb ataladi:

$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}, \quad f'_x, \quad f_x, \quad \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$$

Muhim xususiyatlar:

  • Xususiy hosila olinganda boshqa o'zgaruvchilar o'zgarmas deb olinadi
  • Bir o'zgaruvchili funksiya hosilasi kabi hisoblanadi
  • Har bir o'zgaruvchi bo'yicha alohida hosila topiladi
Misol: \( z = x^2y + \sin(xy) \) $$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = 2xy + y\cos(xy)$$ $$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = x^2 + x\cos(xy)$$

DIFFERENSIALLANUVCHILIK

Ta'rif:

Agar \( f(x, y) \) funksiyaning \( M_0(x_0, y_0) \) nuqtadagi \( \Delta f \) orttirmasi:

$$\Delta f = A\Delta x + B\Delta y + o(\rho)$$

ko'rinishda ifodalansa, funksiya shu nuqtada differensiallanuvchi deyiladi. Bu yerda:

  • \( A, B \) – \( \Delta x, \Delta y \) ga bog'liq bo'lmagan o'zgarmaslar
  • \( \rho = \sqrt{(\Delta x)^2 + (\Delta y)^2} \)
  • \( o(\rho) \) – \( \rho \to 0 \) da nolga intiluvchi kichik hadlar

🔑 Zaruriy shart (1-teorema):

Agar funksiya differensiallanuvchi bo'lsa, u uzluksiz bo'ladi

✅ Etarli shart (3-teorema):

Agar barcha xususiy hosilalar mavjud va uzluksiz bo'lsa, funksiya differensiallanuvchi bo'ladi

TO'LIQ DIFFERENSIAL

Ta'rif:

Funksiya orttirmasining \( \Delta x, \Delta y \) ga nisbatan chiziqli bosh qismi funksiyaning to'liq differensiali deb ataladi:

$$df = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Delta y$$

Yoki qisqacha:

$$df = f'_x dx + f'_y dy$$

Xususiy differensiallar:

$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}dx = f'_x dx, \quad \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}dy = f'_y dy$$

Demak, to'liq differensial = xususiy differensiallar yig'indisi

Misol: \( z = x^3 + xy^2 \) funksiya uchun \( M(1, 2) \) nuqtada: $$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = 3x^2 + y^2 = 3 + 4 = 7$$ $$\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 2xy = 2 \cdot 1 \cdot 2 = 4$$ $$dz = 7dx + 4dy$$

YUQORI TARTIBLI HOSILALAR

Ikkinchi tartibli xususiy hosilalar:

Birinchi tartibli xususiy hosilalardan yana hosila olinsa, ikkinchi tartibli hosilalar hosil bo'ladi:

$$f''_{xx} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2}, \quad f''_{yy} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2}$$ $$f''_{xy} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x \partial y}, \quad f''_{yx} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y \partial x}$$

⭐ Shvarts teoremasi (Aralash hosilalar tenglik):

Agar \( f''_{xy} \) va \( f''_{yx} \) aralash hosilalar uzluksiz bo'lsa: $$f''_{xy} = f''_{yx}$$

Ya'ni, hosilalar olish tartibi muhim emas!

Misol: \( z = x^2y^3 \) $$f'_x = 2xy^3, \quad f'_y = 3x^2y^2$$ $$f''_{xy} = 6xy^2, \quad f''_{yx} = 6xy^2$$

✓ Shvarts teoremasi tasdiqlandi!

TAQRIBIY HISOBLASHGA TATBIQI

Asosiy formula:

Funksiyaning to'liq orttirmasini to'liq differensial bilan almashtirish mumkin:

$$\Delta f \approx df$$ $$f(x, y) \approx f(x_0, y_0) + f'_x(x_0, y_0)(x - x_0) + f'_y(x_0, y_0)(y - y_0)$$

Qo'llanish tartibi:

  1. Hisoblash kerak bo'lgan kattalikni funksiya ko'rinishida ifodalash
  2. Qulaylik uchun \( M_0(x_0, y_0) \) nuqtani tanlash
  3. \( f(x_0, y_0) \) ni hisoblash
  4. Xususiy hosilalarni topish va \( M_0 \) da hisoblash
  5. Formulaga qo'yish
Misol: \( \arctan\left(\frac{1.98}{1.03} - 1\right) \) ni taqribiy hisoblang

Yechim:

1) \( f(x,y) = \arctan\left(\frac{x}{y} - 1\right), \quad x = 1.98, y = 1.03 \)

2) \( x_0 = 2, y_0 = 1 \) deb olamiz

3) \( f(2, 1) = \arctan(1) = \frac{\pi}{4} = 0.785 \)

4) \( f'_x(2,1) = 0.5, \quad f'_y(2,1) = -1 \)

5) \( f(1.98, 1.03) \approx 0.785 + 0.5(-0.02) - 1(0.03) = 0.745 \)

YO'NALISH BO'YICHA HOSILA

Ta'rif:

Funksiyaning ixtiyoriy \( \vec{l} \) yo'nalish bo'yicha o'zgarish tezligi:

$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial \vec{l}} = \lim_{\rho \to 0} \frac{f(M) - f(M_0)}{\rho}$$

Hisoblash formulasi:

$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial \vec{l}} = f'_x \cos\alpha + f'_y \cos\beta$$

Bu yerda \( \cos\alpha, \cos\beta \) – yo'naltiruvchi kosinuslar

⭐ Gradient:

$$\text{grad} f = \nabla f = \left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right)$$

Gradient – funksiyaning eng tez o'sish yo'nalishi!

Fizik ma'no:
  • Harorat maydoni uchun – issiqlik oqimi yo'nalishi
  • Bosim maydoni uchun – shamol yo'nalishi
  • Balandlik maydoni uchun – eng tik ko'tarilish yo'nalishi

TEYLOR FORMULASI

Ko'p o'zgaruvchili funksiya uchun:

$$f(x, y) = f(x_0, y_0) + df(x_0, y_0) + \frac{1}{2!}d^2f(x_0, y_0) + \ldots + \frac{1}{n!}d^nf(\xi, \eta)$$

Bu yerda:

  • \( df \) – birinchi tartibli differensial
  • \( d^2f \) – ikkinchi tartibli differensial
  • \( (\xi, \eta) \) – \( M_0 \) va \( M \) orasidagi nuqta

Ikkinchi tartibli yoyilma:

$$f(x, y) \approx f(x_0, y_0) + f'_x\Delta x + f'_y\Delta y +$$ $$+ \frac{1}{2}(f''_{xx}\Delta x^2 + 2f''_{xy}\Delta x\Delta y + f''_{yy}\Delta y^2)$$
Ahamiyati:
  • Murakkab funksiyalarni ko'phad bilan almashtirish
  • Hisoblashlarni soddalashtirish
  • Xatolarni baholash

EKSTREMUM MASALALARI (1-qism)

Ta'riflar:

Maksimum: Agar \( M_0 \) ning atrofida \( f(M) < f(M_0) \) bo'lsa

Minimum: Agar \( M_0 \) ning atrofida \( f(M) > f(M_0) \) bo'lsa

🔑 Zaruriy shart (Ferma teoremasi):

Agar funksiya \( M_0 \) nuqtada ekstremumga erishsa va xususiy hosilalar mavjud bo'lsa: $$f'_x(M_0) = 0, \quad f'_y(M_0) = 0$$

Statsionar nuqtalar – xususiy hosilalarni nolga aylantiradigan nuqtalar

Misol: \( z = x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y + 5 \)

Statsionar nuqtalarni topamiz:

$$f'_x = 2x - 4 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 2$$ $$f'_y = 2y - 6 = 0 \Rightarrow y = 3$$

Statsionar nuqta: \( M_0(2, 3) \)

EKSTREMUM MASALALARI (2-qism)

✅ Etarli shart (Silvestr alomati):

Statsionar nuqtada ikkinchi tartibli hosilalardan:

$$A = f''_{xx}, \quad B = f''_{xy}, \quad C = f''_{yy}$$ $$\Delta = AC - B^2$$
Shart Natija
\( \Delta > 0 \) va \( A > 0 \) ✓ MINIMUM
\( \Delta > 0 \) va \( A < 0 \) ✓ MAKSIMUM
\( \Delta < 0 \) ✗ EKSTREMUM YO'Q
\( \Delta = 0 \) ? SHUBHALI
